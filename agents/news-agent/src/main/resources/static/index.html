News Agent

AI-powered supply-chain news classification. Fetches articles from mockServices, enriches with cities from location-service, and classifies via Claude Haiku.

GET /api/agent/classified-news

Returns classified articles with title, locations (array of where the news is happening, AI-extracted from body), and topics. Requires mockServices, location-service, and ANTHROPIC_API_KEY.
